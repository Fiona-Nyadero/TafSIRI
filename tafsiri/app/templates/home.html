What's TafSIRI?

The TafSIRI Initiative is an online, innovative Public Participation platform for public infrastructure projects in Kenya.

TafSIRI is where you see all public projects, displayed in a user-friendly way.

'Tafsiri', means 'to decode/interpret/translate.'

Why TafSIRI?

The current approach to public participation in infrastructure development in Kenya is failing.

Public participation is considered as one of the principles of good governance by the 2010 Kenyan Constitution.

TafSIRI calls out the effectiveness and quality of public participation in the country.
