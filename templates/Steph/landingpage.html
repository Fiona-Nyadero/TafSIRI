TafSIRI is where you see all architectural projects

The Digital Baraza

  • The TafSIRI Initiative is a proposed informative and interactive platform geared towards increasing the effectiveness of Public Participation in infrastructure projects at County level.
  • TafSIRI is a play on the Swahili word ‘Tafsiri’ which means ‘to decode/interpret/translate’.
  • Leveraging on technology, the project will interpret proposed complex Architectural/Engineering plans and specifications to the Public, collect and relay public feedback to the County Governments.
  • TafSIRI will transform the quality of public participation in the country; empowering people to make informed decisions regarding infrastructure developments in their municipalities and cities.
